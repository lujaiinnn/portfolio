Customer Analysis Dashboard

"Unlocking Customer Value with Smart Segmentation & Lifetime Insights"

1. Introduction to Customer Analysis Dashboard: In today’s hyper-competitive marketplace, customer-centric strategies are vital for sustainable growth. The Customer Analysis Dashboard created using Tableau enables a deep dive into customer behavior, segmentation, profitability, and lifetime value. This dashboard equips business leaders with data-backed insights to drive loyalty and revenue.

Key features of the dashboard include:
- Customer segmentation by demographics and geography
- Sales, profit, and order volume per customer
- Customer lifetime value estimation
- Top customer profiling and purchase behavior

2. Understanding Customer Demographics:
This section presents a breakdown of customers based on age, gender, location, and segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).
Insights:
- Corporate customers showed the highest average profit per order.
- Consumer segment accounted for the highest sales volume.

3. Customer Purchase Frequency:
Visualizations show order frequency per customer and seasonal trends. Repeat vs. one-time buyers are easily distinguishable.
Insights:
- A small portion of customers account for a large share of repeat purchases.
- Q4 consistently shows higher purchase frequency across years.

4. Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV):
This combines average order value, purchase frequency, and profit per customer.
Insights:
- High CLTV customers are from the Technology category.
- The top 20% of customers generated over 60% of profits.

5. Customer Segmentation:
RFM analysis segments customers into Champions, Loyal, New, At Risk, and Lost.
Insights:
- “Champions” make frequent, high-value recent purchases.
- “At Risk” segment is notable and needs win-back campaigns.

6. Regional Customer Behavior:
This section shows customer data by region with a map and filters.
Insights:
- West and South regions are the most profitable.
- Urban areas show higher return rates.
Strategy: Geo-targeted marketing can enhance performance.

7. High-Value Customer Profiling:
Profiles of the top 10 highest revenue customers include order value, frequency, and history.
Insights:
- Top buyers favor Furniture and Technology categories.
- Their average profit margin exceeds 30%.

8. Final Summary and Recommendations:
Key Takeaways:
1. Corporate segment yields higher profits.
2. Loyal and high-CLTV customers are key drivers.
3. 70%+ profit from just 25% of customers.
4. Urban regions need return rate management.

Recommendations:
- Personalize marketing by segment.
- Expand in high-value regions.
