Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard

"Visualizing Retail Insights with Power BI"

This project focuses on analyzing Adidas sales data using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into revenue, profit, product performance, and regional sales. It helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve strategy, performance, and forecasting.

Objective: The goal of this dashboard is to identify high-performing products, track regional sales trends, analyze profitability, and recommend business improvements based on the data.

Dataset Overview: The dataset includes order date, region, retailer, product type, units sold, revenue, cost, and profit for Adidas products sold globally.

Data Preparation: The data was cleaned in Power Query by removing nulls, formatting dates, creating calculated fields like Profit Margin, and extracting Month and Year for time-based analysis.

KPI Dashboard: The main page displays key performance indicators such as Total Revenue, Total Profit, Units Sold, and Profit Margin. Line charts and pie charts help visualize trends and product share.

Regional Insights: A map and bar chart compare revenue and profit across regions and retailers. Europe and North America were identified as top-performing markets.

Product Analysis: A treemap and clustered bar chart highlight the best-selling and most profitable product types. Footwear consistently emerged as the most profitable category.

Profitability Analysis: Using waterfall charts and profit margin tables, the dashboard reveals gaps between revenue and actual profit, uncovering products with high revenue but low profitability.

Conclusion: The Power BI dashboard for Adidas sales analysis empowers business teams with a visual tool to track, compare, and understand sales metrics. It supports smart planning, inventory control, and better retail decisions.
